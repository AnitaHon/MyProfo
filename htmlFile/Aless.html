Alessi Rebrand

Description

The Alessi rebrand focuses on elevating the brand's presence in Canada by enhancing brand awareness and recognition among Canadian Millennials and Gen X consumers. Featuring a sleek, minimalist design, the new logo incorporates elements like a chopping board and a fork to reinforce Alessi's dedication to culinary excellence. The campaign's message, "If you know, you know," highlights the brand’s design quality and craftsmanship, inviting customers to discover the unique functionality and aesthetic of Alessi products. Through strategic use of OOH advertising, social media content, and a strong community presence, the rebrand aims to resonate with consumers who value modern design and curated living spaces.

Skill

Adobe InDesign, Illustrator, PhotoShop
