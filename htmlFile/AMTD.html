AMTD

Description

For this UX/UI project, I designed the digital experience for AMTD, a cutting-edge brand that specializes in cyberpunk-themed athletic apparel, particularly for boarding enthusiasts. AMTD aims to inspire change and creativity by challenging the norm and empowering individuals to express their uniqueness through innovative, high-performance designs.

The goal was to create an immersive online experience that reflects the brand’s bold and futuristic ethos. Through dynamic visuals, an intuitive interface, and a seamless user journey, I crafted a platform where users can easily explore AMTD’s products while feeling connected to the brand’s vision of creativity and self-expression. The design integrates cyberpunk aesthetics, emphasizing vibrant colors, futuristic elements, and a high-energy vibe that enhances the brand's identity.

AMTD's platform invites individuals to break free from the ordinary, encouraging them to express their creativity and elevate their performance, both on and off the board.

Skill

Adobe InDesign, Illustrator, PhotoShop
