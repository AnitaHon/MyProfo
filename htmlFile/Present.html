Present

Description

This animation brings to life one of the most iconic and inspiring quotes from Kung Fu Panda: "Yesterday is history, tomorrow is a mystery, but today is a gift. That is why it is called the present." — Master Oogway.

The animation visually interprets the message of living in the present moment, encouraging viewers to embrace today as a gift, free from the weight of the past or the uncertainty of the future. Through dynamic motion and vibrant visuals, the piece emphasizes the power of mindfulness and the importance of appreciating the now.

Skill

Adobe Illustrator, After Effects
